About Rox Pet Palace

At Rox Pet Palace, we strive to provide your furry friends with the royal grooming services that they deserve.

Founded in 2025, our goal is to ensure your dog are taken care of with the finest pet safe products on the market. Our experienced pet groomers provide top-notch, personalized care to exactly what your pet needs.

We want to create an environment that is more than just a clean up or a haircut, but something that your best friend can look forward to, and a place where pet parents can rest assured their dog is in good hands.
